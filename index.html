⚔️ Lets Wipe PvE Guild !

Let's Wipe is an international guild managed by experienced leadership focused on clearing everything in the game in a timely manner. We are together from week 1 of TBC. Trying to form a healthy community, the aspect that ultimately we enjoy most.

⚔️ What are we looking for?

Motivated people who will attend to our raiding days and aim for as close to 100% attendance as possible, a few misses here and there isn't a big issue as long as we are made aware of your absence. We expect you to have knowledge of the raids & tactics, to be geared, appropriate for your class and role before taking part in our raid team.

⚔️ Raid Schedule ⚔️:

Wednesday - Sunday 19:00 ST

You are expected to have WoW experience Coming fully prepared to all encounters - including consumes, enchants, gear and tactics Being able to communicate clearly in English on voice or chat Have a progress orientated mindset, where you focus on improving whilst prioritizing mechanics over logs, but must have logs best avg over 80, to show a general idea of encounters performance so far
